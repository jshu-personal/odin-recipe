Pork Katsu Curry

Description

This delicious, easy-to-make concoction of meat and gelatin will get your mouth watering in no time.

Coming together in <30 minutes, you'll have this one on rotation for all your lazy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • Curry blocks
  • Pork cutlets
  • Rice
  • Panko
  • Onion
  • Chicken broth
  • Flour
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Pound out the pork cutlets.
  2. Drench the cutlets in flour and beated eggs.
  3. In a separate pot, cook onions.
  4. Add chicken broth to pot.
  5. With a neutral oil, fry the pork cutlets.
  6. Prepare rice in a cooker.
  7. Put it all together, and enjoy!
